Brief overview of program:

Overview:
Draws a board of backgammon

1. Draws the canvas and the background elements
	1.1 Creates a blank brown canvas
	1.2 Creates a middle divider line
	1.3 Creates the beige left and right playing area

2. Draws the triangle places
	2.1 Determines whether the triangle belongs on the top left or the top right
	2.2 Determines whether the triangle is dark orange or tan
	2.3 Layers all the top triangles together, clones them, and flips them over to the other side to create the bottom triangle playing area

3. Draw the black and white playing pieces by by determining whether the white is on the top or on the bottom
	3.1 If on top, then draws the white pieces on top and black on bottom
	3.2 If on bottom, then draws the black pieces on top and the white on bottom
